Company Vision

Trackcare Integrated Services Ltd is a real estate design, construction, maintenance and management company. Based in Abia State, we run projects in all the eastern states of Nigeria.
We help organizations and individuals maximize value by providing them with world-class standard design, construction, maintenance, remodeling and real estate management services at competitive prices. Our broad customer base and project portfolio covers a wide range of clientele including corporate, residential, hospitality, administrative, diplomatic and recreational services and projects as well as landscapes, swimming pools, lightings, home remodeling and renovation services.

Our Approach

The projects range from single build-and-deliver projects to long-term maintenance and development contracts. Our clients include large and small scale establishments, States, Oil Companies, Hotels, Corporate outfits and private individuals.

How we work

  • Highest Quality Standard Our main focus is customer satisfaction. To achieve that, our team of architects, project engineers, quality control engineers and skilled contractors ensure highest quality standards. Along with this, we also ensure the use of branded materials for the project.
  • Hassle-Free Experience Once the deal is sealed, every step from plan approval to handover will be our responsibility, managed 100% by our qualified team. Our tech capabilities does wonders in taking a customer’s doubts and stress away from the entire process. No more hassle of dealing with contractors and worry of execution issues.
  • On-Time Completion We provide a master schedule and monthly schedules, and commit to completing projects on time. To ensure clients are aware of the progress, weekly reports are consistently shared. We also follow a penalty policy in case of any delay.
